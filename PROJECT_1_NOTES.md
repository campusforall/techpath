# Project 1: TechPath A/L Technology Resource Hub

## Purpose

This is a static, attractive, mobile-responsive website for Sri Lankan A/L Technology students.

The site is intended to share study resources such as:

- Past papers
- Textbooks
- Teachers' guides
- Model papers
- Province papers
- Question Wise papers
- Extra Notes

Resources are managed through Google Drive links in `outputs/tech-study-hub/resources.js`.

## Current Website Location

Main site:

`index.html`


## Subjects Included

- Engineering Technology
- Science for Technology
- Information & Communication Technology
- Bio Systems Technology
- Agricultural Science
